Bring Our Mobile Bar to Your Event

Cruise Cart is a mobile bartending and coffee catering service serving the greater DFW area — Fort Worth, Burleson, Crowley, and surrounding cities. Our white bar setup and mobile tap truck arrive directly at your event, fully equipped with professional bartenders and everything you need for an unforgettable experience. Every drink is crafted with care, and every setup is designed to complement your celebration.

Elevate Any Event with Mobile Bar Catering

Enjoy a full mobile bar experience including; professional bartenders, cups, napkins, straws, basic garnishes, tajin & chamoy rims, a customized drink menu, and a personalized shopping list for mixers, alcohol, and ice. Dry hire bar — we bring the setup, you bring the spirits.
